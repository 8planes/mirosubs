Open Subtitles Design Sprint

The Open Subtitles Design Sprint will bring together a group of global stakeholders in the subtitling space for a tightly focused / goal oriented meeting.

Meeting Goals

  • Map out current subtitling needs of stakeholders, including content creators and video services.
  • Discuss ways to solve these needs, identify existing technology that can be leveraged, and be aware of logical spaces for collaboration/cooperation.
  • Move towards standardizing these solutions and pushing forward the state of the art in open subtiting.
  • Connect various stakeholders working on projects with open subtitling needs or focus.

Participants

Broadly, the participants will include: video producers, video hosting services, open source developers, standards oriented engineers, open courseware producers, and other individuals and organizations with a stake in the subtitling world. We'll make a roster available when the event draws closer.

Attendees are selected by invitation; however, if you or your organization would like to participate, please contact: universalsubtitles@pculture.org

When & Where?

The meeting will be held in the Fall of 2010 in New York City. The exact date and location are TBA.
