Universal Subtitles in Action

Khan Academy is a not-for-profit with the goal of changing education for the better by providing a free world-class education to anyone anywhere. They decided their 1,600+ educational videos need to be available in all of the world’s languages.

Using Universal Subtitles, all of their videos are easily translatable. They have a growing volunteer community translating new videos every day.
