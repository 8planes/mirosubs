Universal Subtitles

Make your video truly global & multi-cultural

Universal Subtitles is a platform for video translation and subtitling—it’s powered by volunteer collaborators, much like Wikipedia.

The software is open source and developed by a non-profit organization.

Benefits of Universal Subtitles

  • Language barriers become obsolete
  • Very different cultures communicate directly
  • Global connection through video


More reasons to use Universal Subtitles

  • Higher search engine rankings (SEO)
  • Accessibility for the deaf and hard of hearing
  • We help you build volunteer translation capacity

Universal Subtitles in Action

Khan Academy is a not-for-profit with the goal of changing education for the better by providing a free world-class education to anyone anywhere. They decided their 1,600+ educational videos need to be available in all of the world’s languages.

Using Universal Subtitles, all of their videos are easily translatable. They have a growing volunteer community translating new videos every day.
