Collaborative subtitles and captions, in your browser

Welcome to the Universal Subtitles addon for Firefox. How the addon works: Universal Subtitles offers a javascript widget you can use to make any video subtitle-able.

The addon puts this subtitle widget around every compatible video in every page you visit.

If somebody in our community has already submitted subtitles for a video, youʼll see them. You can also contribute your own subtitles and translations!

A "subtitle me" or "choose language" button will appear across all compatible videos. Click the button to choose or create subtitles.

 

Note: this addon is an experimental part of the Universal Subtitles service. If you run into problems, just disabled the addon and contact us, donʼt give us a bad rating :) Known issues:

  • Most Flash videos (e.g. Youtube) will be set to a default, small size
  • Blip.TV and some other sitesʼ videos will play in our custom player
  • Our community is just getting started, so you may not find many already-subtitled videos. For videos you like, make your own!

Any other problems, especially styling problems on the page, contact us and include the site URL!
