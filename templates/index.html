With Universal Subtitles, you can

Add subtitles

We've built the easiest way to subtitle videos. Add our subtitling widget to your video (or your site) and click the "subtitle me" button. The rest is simple! Totally free, and no software to download.

Add translations

Once subtitles exist, any viewer can add a translation in any language. We also use Google Translate for instant machine translation between dozens of languages. Reach an international audience!

Get volunteer help

Your viewers (and members of our subtitling community) can add translations and make improvements. That way subtitles are constantly improving - like wikipedia. We organize your international audience to make your videos more accessible.

 

It’s free with no software to download

Add our embed code to your videos. Submit your video, and get an embed code to post in your site. When the page loads, our subtitle tools will display underneath your video (as a small, simple button)

Subtitling tools open in your page. When someone clicks the “subtitle me” button, a subtitling interface appears on your page (most users never leave your site). Subtitles they create are then visible to other users, on your site.

How it works

A community of volunteers improving your subtitles Each video gets its own collaboration space on our site (like a wikipedia article) where users can make improvements, track changes, and give each other feedback.

 

Why Universal Subtitles is different

  • It's built by a 501(c)3 nonprofit working with a community of volunteers (find out how to volunteer).
  • The code is free and open source (get the source code)
  • It's built using Javascript and HTML5 instead of proprietary software like Flash
  • It's 100% web-based, there’s no software to download.
  • You don't have to host your video with us, and you're not dependent on any one video host, platform, or format.
  • People are free to add subtitles to any video
