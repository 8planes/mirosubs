Subtitles and Captions for Every Video on the Web

The Problem Web video is beginning to rival television, but there isn't a good open resource for subtitling. Our Mission We're trying to make captioning, subtitling, and translating video publicly accessible in a way that's free and open, just like the Web.

Subtitle Widget

A simple way to create and add captions to almost any video on the web (without having to transcode or re-upload videos).

Subtitle Lookup Protocol

An open standard to let Firefox extensions, desktop video players, websites, browsers--any client--find and retrieve subtitles for any video.

Community Site

An online community for collaboratively subtitling and translating the world's videos (like a Wikipedia for subtitles).
