Step 1: Transcription

In this step, you'll be typing a transcript containing everything spoken in the video.
  • When people start speaking, type all words they say.
  • Hit [ENTER] after each subtitle.
  • The video will get ahead of you. Use the [CTRL] key to skip back 4 seconds.

Tips:

  • Don't worry about timing, we'll take care of that in the next step. Just type!
  • Hit [enter] twice for silence, if there's a break in the dialog.
  • Don't make any subtitles too long! One line (in the entry area) is about right.

Step 2: Synching

Now, you'll synchronize the subtitles that you have transcribed in the previous step.
  • Hit [SPACEBAR] when the first line should appear on screen.
  • Everytime you hit [SPACEBAR] the next line is displayed, until the end of the transcribed lines.

Tips:

  • You still can use the [CTRL] key to skip back 4 seconds and watch the movie with the subtitles you have synchronized so far.
